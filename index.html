Who are we?

We are a group of passionate educators, designers and developers looking to innovate news reading for young children.

Our main aim is curating and summarising positive, fun and engaging news for our young ones, giving them hope and faith even in the midst of all the negativity.

To our children, the world can be a huge and scary place. Through our news, we believe that we can help children discover new knowledge about the world around them, understand that the world is their oyster and be empowered to make the world a better place - one step at a time.
